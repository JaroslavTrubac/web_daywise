🗑 How to delete your data

Daywise does not require an account and stores most data locally on your device. To remove all data:

  1. Open iOS Settings → scroll down to Daywise
  2. Tap "Delete App" — this removes the app and all locally stored preferences and cached weather data
  3. Confirm deletion when prompted

If you have concerns about any other data (e.g. from crash reporting), email us at jaroslav.trubac@gmail.com with the subject line "Data deletion request" and we will process it within 30 days in line with GDPR.
