2 Description of service

Daywise is a weather-intelligence application that translates current and forecasted weather conditions into practical daily guidance across three dimensions:

  • Move – activity and outdoor exercise recommendations based on weather conditions.
  • Feel – information about how weather (pressure, humidity, temperature) may affect your physical well-being.
  • Live – smart decisions for the day: what to wear, whether to carry an umbrella, when to schedule outdoor plans.

The service relies on third-party weather data and is provided on an "as is" basis. Weather forecasting is inherently uncertain and recommendations may not always be accurate.
