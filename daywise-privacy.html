1 Who we are

Daywise is a mobile application that translates weather forecasts into practical daily guidance across three areas: movement (Move), physical well-being (Feel), and daily planning (Live).

The app is developed and operated by Jaroslav Trubač ("we", "us", or "our"), reachable at jaroslav.trubac@gmail.com.

For users in the European Union, we act as the data controller under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2 Data we collect

Data type What exactly Why
Location Approximate or precise GPS coordinates (only when the app is in use) To fetch local weather conditions relevant to your day
Weather data Fetched from a third-party weather API based on your location Core app function – weather drives all recommendations
App preferences Settings, notification preferences, activity preferences To personalise your brief and remember your choices
Crash & diagnostic data Anonymous crash reports, app version, device OS version To fix bugs and improve stability

We do not collect: your name, email address, phone number, photos, contacts, health records, financial data, or browsing history.

Daywise does not require you to create an account.

3 How we use your data

We use the data described above solely to:

  • Deliver weather-based daily recommendations (Move, Feel, Live)
  • Send optional daily briefing notifications (only if you opt in)
  • Remember your in-app settings across sessions
  • Diagnose crashes and improve app performance

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the core app functionality.

Legal basis under GDPR: Legitimate interest (providing the service you requested) and consent (for notifications and location access, which you can withdraw at any time).

4 Third-party services

To provide accurate weather data and maintain app quality, we use the following third-party services:

  • Weather API provider – receives your approximate location coordinates to return weather data. No personal identifiers are transmitted.
  • Apple App Store / TestFlight – subject to Apple's own privacy policy when downloading or updating the app.
  • Crash reporting tool (if applicable) – receives anonymised diagnostic data only.

We do not integrate advertising SDKs, social media tracking pixels, or data broker services.

5 Data storage & retention

Most data (weather cache, preferences) is stored locally on your device and never leaves it except as described in Section 4.

Crash and diagnostic data, if collected, is retained for a maximum of 12 months and then automatically deleted.

Location data is used in real time to fetch weather and is not stored on our servers.

You can delete all locally stored app data at any time by uninstalling the app from your device.

6 Your rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access – you can request a copy of any personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification – you can ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to erasure – you can ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Right to restriction – you can ask us to limit how we use your data.
  • Right to data portability – you can request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object – you can object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Right to withdraw consent – you can revoke location or notification permissions at any time in your device settings.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at jaroslav.trubac@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority (in the Czech Republic: Úřad pro ochranu osobních údajů, uoou.cz).

7 Children's privacy

Daywise is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in certain EU member states). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at jaroslav.trubac@gmail.com and we will delete it promptly.

8 Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.

For significant changes, we will notify you via an in-app notice or push notification (if you have them enabled) at least 14 days before the change takes effect.

Continued use of the app after the effective date constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
